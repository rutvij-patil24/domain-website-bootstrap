Webinars

Maths and Neural Networks

  • Speaker:Ms Mithila Harish
  • Event date: 06 March 2020
  • Event link: https://youtu.be/Cj887Fxk9w0
  • Event description:

    More than 800 students registered for the webinar which discusses how industry approaches machine learning (ml) - in particular neural network (nn) solutions. It provided a practical overview of the nn solution landscape. It also helped to indicate the role of basic loss functions (sigmoid etc) and nonlinear activatigon functions. The students were able to become familiar with data sampling issues.



Mathematical Intuition- From engineering to Innovation

  • Speaker:Mr Shivam Mishra
  • Event date: 09 June, 2020
  • Event link: https://youtu.be/mVE2WhxAkP4
  • Event description:

    The webinar informed how maths influences various technological fields like ML, AI.It provided a practical overview of the concept of intergration and differentiation. It indicated the role of Fourier series in the world of artificial intelligence.It helped students to become familiar with concepts behind pi.



New Age Learning - How to conduct Sessions Online

  • Speaker:Mr Shreyas Kulkarni and Mr. Bhushan Kolhe
  • Event date: 14 June, 2020
  • Event Link: https://youtu.be/jLdXbZUUoG0
  • Event description:

    This event was conducted by the students for the faculty staff. The resource people spoke about conducting lectures on various platforms, streaming the lectures on YouTube. The participants were also informed about creating Google forms and linking the forms to create a certificate or email.
